Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions homeowners and property managers ask most.

How quickly can you respond to a water damage emergency?

We guarantee a response time of 45 minutes or less, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week including weekends and holidays. Our teams are strategically stationed across the GTA and Ontario to reach you fast.

Do you work directly with insurance companies?

Yes. We coordinate directly with your insurance provider, handling all documentation, reporting, and claims support so you don't have to manage two crises at once. We provide formal documentation for every job.

What areas do you service across Ontario?

We serve the entire GTA and broader Ontario region including Toronto, Mississauga, Brampton, Markham, Richmond Hill, Vaughan, Oakville, Burlington, Hamilton, Barrie, Newmarket, and hundreds more communities across Ontario.

Are your technicians certified?

Yes. All 247 Restoration technicians are fully certified and trained in water damage restoration, mould remediation, biohazard cleanup, asbestos abatement, and other specialized services. We use advanced equipment and proven techniques.

Do you offer free estimates?

Yes. We provide free on-call estimates. Call us at 416-993-1111 any time or fill out our online form to request a free assessment of your situation. There is no obligation to proceed.

What should I do while waiting for the restoration team?

If safe to do so: turn off the water source if it is a plumbing issue, remove valuables from affected areas, avoid using electrical appliances near standing water, and document the damage with photos for your insurance claim. Do not enter areas with sewage backup or biohazard contamination — wait for our team.
